### Q1. Quantitative logic question for arrays_coding?
**Question**: What is the unit digit of 7^53 + 3^46?
A) 0  
B) 2  
C) 4  
D) 6  
**Answer**: `D`

#### Explanation:
- 7^53: Cyclicity of 7 is 4. 53 % 4 = 1. Unit digit = 7.
- 3^46: Cyclicity of 3 is 4. 46 % 4 = 2. Unit digit = 9.
- Sum = 7 + 9 = 16. Unit digit is 6.
