## 💡 Prep Strategy for TCS NQT Verbal
1. **Time Management**: The Verbal section consists of 20 questions in 20 minutes. Speed is essential. Read the questions *before* reading the RC passage.
2. **Grammar Rules**: Over 50% of Error Spotting and Fill in the Blanks questions test **Subject-Verb Agreement** and **Prepositions**. Master these rules.
3. **Clue Words in Jumbles**: Look for transition words like *However*, *Therefore*, *Consequently*, *Furthermore*, which signal the position of a sentence relative to others.
