## Previous Year Questions

### Q1. Choose the word closest in meaning to: **BENEVOLENT**
A) Malevolent  
B) Magnanimous  
C) Miserly  
D) Malicious  
**Answer**: `B`  
**Explanation**: "Benevolent" means kind, generous, and charitable. "Magnanimous" means generous or forgiving, which is closest in meaning. "Malevolent" and "Malicious" are antonyms (meaning harmful/evil), and "Miserly" means cheap/stingy.

### Q2. Choose the word opposite in meaning to: **EPHEMERAL**
A) Transient  
B) Perpetual  
C) Evanescent  
D) Fleeting  
**Answer**: `B`  
**Explanation**: "Ephemeral" means lasting for a very short time. Its antonym is "Perpetual" which means everlasting or permanent. "Transient", "Evanescent", and "Fleeting" are synonyms.
