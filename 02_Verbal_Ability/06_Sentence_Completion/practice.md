## 🟢 Beginner Level

**Q1.** The speaker's _____ delivery captivated the audience throughout the seminar. (Variant 1)
A) monotone
B) eloquent
C) boring
D) harsh
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `B`
* **Explanation**: Captivated implies a positive, engaging delivery, hence eloquent.

</details>
