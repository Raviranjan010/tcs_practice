## ⚠️ EXAM TRAPS
- Trap 1: Collective nouns treated incorrectly as plural.
- Trap 2: Mismatched tenses in conditional clauses.
- Trap 3: Faulty pronoun reference.
- Trap 4: Misplaced modifiers.
- Trap 5: Confusing prepositions.
