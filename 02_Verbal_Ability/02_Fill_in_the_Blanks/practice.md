**Q1.** Although the initial results were _____, the team remained _____ about the final outcome. (Variant 1)
A) discouraging / pessimistic
B) promising / skeptical
C) discouraging / optimistic
D) stellar / hopeful
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `C`
* **Explanation**: Although signals contrast. Discouraging and optimistic provide contrast.
* **Detailed Step**: Apply grammar/context rules.
</details>
