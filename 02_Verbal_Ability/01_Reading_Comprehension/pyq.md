## [2024] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Ninja]
**Question 1**:
Based on the text: 'The rise of electric vehicles (EVs) has triggered a shift in battery technology. Although lithium-ion remains the standard, solid-state batteries are gaining traction as they offer faster charging.' Which of the following is true?

**Options**:
- A) Lithium-ion is no longer used.
- B) Solid-state batteries are the current standard.
- C) Solid-state batteries offer faster charging.
- D) EVs are failing.

**Correct Answer**: **C) Solid-state batteries offer faster charging.**

### Explanation:
The text directly states: 'solid-state batteries are gaining traction as they offer faster charging.' This directly maps to Option C.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Direct scanning. The text explicitly says solid-state batteries 'offer faster charging.' Match keywords to verify C instantly.

## [2023] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Ninja]
**Question 2**:
Select the most appropriate option to fill in the blank: 'The new manager resolved the conflicts _______ tact and diplomacy.'

**Options**:
- A) with
- B) by
- C) at
- D) in

**Correct Answer**: **A) with**

### Explanation:
The preposition 'with' indicates the means or tool used ('tact and diplomacy') to achieve an outcome.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Substitute prepositions contextually. 'Resolved with tact' flows naturally and represents agency correctly.
