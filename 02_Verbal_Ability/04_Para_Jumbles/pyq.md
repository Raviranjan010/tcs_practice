## [2024] [DIFFICULTY: Hard] [CATEGORY: Digital]
**Question 1**:
Rearrange the sentences:
1) AI models require high computation power.
2) Therefore, companies are investing in specialized hardware.
3) This computing infrastructure is expensive to run.
4) GPUs have become the standard for training these models.

**Options**:
- A) 1-4-3-2
- B) 1-3-2-4
- C) 4-1-3-2
- D) 1-2-3-4

**Correct Answer**: **A) 1-4-3-2**

### Explanation:
Sentence 1 introduces the topic of computation power in AI. Sentence 4 specifies that GPUs are the standard for training. Sentence 3 ('This computing infrastructure...') refers to the GPUs and computation power mentioned in 1 and 4. Sentence 2 ('Therefore...') concludes the paragraph with the business consequence. Order is 1-4-3-2.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Look for reference links. 'This computing infrastructure' in 3 refers to GPUs in 4. So 4-3 is a mandatory pair. Only Option A has 4-3 and starts with 1.

## [2023] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Ninja]
**Question 2**:
Select the most appropriate option to fill in the blank: 'The new manager resolved the conflicts _______ tact and diplomacy.'

**Options**:
- A) with
- B) by
- C) at
- D) in

**Correct Answer**: **A) with**

### Explanation:
The preposition 'with' indicates the means or tool used ('tact and diplomacy') to achieve an outcome.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Substitute prepositions contextually. 'Resolved with tact' flows naturally and represents agency correctly.
