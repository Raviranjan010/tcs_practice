## 🟢 Beginner Level (Questions 1 - 10)

**Q1.** Find the error: 'Each of the 1 servers are monitored continuously.'
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `are -> is`
* **Method**: Subject-verb agreement: 'Each' is singular and takes 'is'.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  'Each' is singular. 'servers' is plural but the subject is 'Each'. So use 'is'.
* **Target Time**: 15 seconds
</details>

<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `since -> for`
* **Method**: Preposition rule: duration takes 'for', start point takes 'since'.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  '{i} hours' is a duration of time, hence 'for' should be used instead of 'since'.

**Q21.** Find the error: 'Not only the manager but also the developer are going to attend the review.'
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `are -> is`
* **Method**: Subject-verb agreement: verb agrees with the closer subject 'developer' (singular).
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  With 'Not only... but also', the verb agrees with the closer subject, which is 'developer' (singular). So 'are' should be 'is'.
* **Target Time**: 20 seconds
</details>
