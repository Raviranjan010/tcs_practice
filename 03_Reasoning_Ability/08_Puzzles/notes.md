## ⚠️ EXAM TRAPS
- Trap 1: Assuming gender in blood relations.
- Trap 2: Outward facing left/right directions in circular seating.
- Trap 3: Non-leap century years (e.g. 1900).
- Trap 4: Overlapping Venn diagram boundary states.
- Trap 5: Misidentifying odd days in calendar calculations.

## 🔥 60-SECOND SHORTCUTS
1. Write down alphabet positions (A-Z and Z-A) at the beginning of the exam.
2. Focus on fixed placements in seating layouts first.
3. Use Venn Diagrams for syllogisms instead of logical equations.
