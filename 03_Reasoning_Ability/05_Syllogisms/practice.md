**Q11.** Statements: Some dogs are friendly. All friendly animals are pets. Conclusion: Are some dogs pets?
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `Yes / Follows`
* **Method**: Venn diagram intersection.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Dogs overlaps Friendly. Friendly is entirely inside Pets. Hence, the overlapping part of Dogs must be inside Pets.

## 🔴 Advanced Level (Questions 21 - 30)

**Q21.** Statements: No cat is mouse. All mice are rodents. Conclusion: I) Some rodents are mice. II) No cat is rodent. Does II follow?
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `No / Does not follow`
* **Method**: Venn diagram alternate case checking.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Rodents can overlap with Cat while keeping Mouse separate. Thus, 'No cat is rodent' is not universally true.
* **Target Time**: 30 seconds
</details>
