## [TCS NQT] Question 1
If DREAM is coded as FTGCO, what is the coding for SLEEP?
A) UNGGP
B) UJGGP
C) TKGGQ
D) TMGGQ
**Answer**: `A`

### Explanation:
- D (+2) = F
- R (+2) = T
- E (+2) = G
- A (+2) = C
- M (+2) = O
Applying the same +2 shift to SLEEP:
- S (+2) = U
- L (+2) = N
- E (+2) = G
- E (+2) = G
- P (+2) = R
Therefore, SLEEP is coded as UNGGP.
