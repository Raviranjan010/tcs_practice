# 🧠 Reasoning Shortcuts & Tricks

This document compiles the quickest visual and logical methods to solve TCS NQT reasoning questions.

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Blood Relations Family Tree Symbols

Use this standard representation to construct diagrams rapidly:

* **Male**: Square box or $(+)$ sign.
* **Female**: Circle or $(-)$ sign.
* **Husband-Wife (Spouses)**: Double horizontal lines ($=$).
* **Siblings**: Single horizontal line ($-$).
* **Generations**: Vertical lines ($|$).

## ⭕ Circular Seating Arrangements

* **Facing Inward**: 
  - Clockwise = Left turn.
  - Counter-Clockwise = Right turn.
* **Facing Outward**:
  - Clockwise = Right turn.
  - Counter-Clockwise = Left turn.

> [!TIP]
> Always place the first person at the **6 o'clock position (bottom)**. This makes 'facing inward' left/right matches your own physical left/right.

## 📅 Calendar Hand Angles & Odd Days

### Odd Days Table (Remainder of days divided by 7)
* **Ordinary Year (365 days)**: $365 \equiv 1 \text{ odd day}$.
* **Leap Year (366 days)**: $366 \equiv 2 \text{ odd days}$.
* **Centuries Odd Days**:
  - 100 Years = 5 odd days
  - 200 Years = 3 odd days
  - 300 Years = 1 odd day
  - 400 Years = 0 odd days (repeats every 400 years)

### Clocks Formulas
* **Angle between hour & minute hand at H hours and M minutes**: 
  $$\theta = \left| 30H - \frac{11}{2}M \right|$$
* **Coincidence of hands**: Hands overlap every $65 \frac{5}{11}$ minutes.
