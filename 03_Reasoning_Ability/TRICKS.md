# 🧠 Reasoning Ability Tricks & Shortcuts

Master these quick tricks to solve TCS NQT logical reasoning questions in under 45 seconds.

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Blood Relations Family Tree Symbols

Always draw a generation tree top-down, with older generations at the top.
* **Male**: [+] sign or Square box
* **Female**: [-] sign or Circle
* **Spouses**: Double horizontal lines (=)
* **Siblings**: Single horizontal line (-)
* **Generations**: Vertical lines (|)

---

## ⭕ Circular Seating Arrangements

* **Facing Inward**: Clockwise is Left, Counter-Clockwise is Right.
* **Facing Outward**: Clockwise is Right, Counter-Clockwise is Left.
Draw an axis diagram to place candidates precisely. If linear, mark North/South directions.

---

## 📅 Clock Angle & Calendar Tricks

- **Angle between hands**: $\theta = |30H - 5.5M|$ where H is hour, M is minute.
- **Hands Coincide**: Hands coincide every $65\frac{5}{11}$ minutes (not 65 minutes).
- **Odd Days Method**: Add odd days: Jan=3, Feb=0/1, Mar=3, Apr=2... divide total by 7. Remainder gives the day.

---

## 🗳️ Syllogisms Small Venn Check

- Draw a small, minimal-overlap Venn diagram first.
- Check if the conclusion region is always shaded or overlapping in all possible representations.
- If it is doubtful in even one scenario, the conclusion is invalid.

---

## 🔠 Coding-Decoding Rankings

- Write down the alphabet A-Z and reverse Z-A above/below each other.
- Use **EJOTY** (5, 10, 15, 20, 25) to recall ranks instantly.
- Check for alternate shifts, reversals, or sum of letters.

---

## 📊 Data Interpretation Speed Hacks

- Calculate percentage change using the ratio multiplier: $\% \text{ change} = \frac{\text{New} - \text{Old}}{\text{Old}} \times 100$.
- Bookmark the formula: $\% \text{ share} = \frac{\text{Value}}{\text{Total}} \times 100$.
- Approximate fractions: $\frac{43}{81} \approx \frac{40}{80} = 50\%$.
