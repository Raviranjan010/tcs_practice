## [2024] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Ninja]
**Question 1**:
Pointing to a man, a woman said, 'His mother is the only daughter of my mother.' How is the woman related to the man?

**Options**:
- A) Mother
- B) Sister
- C) Grandmother
- D) Daughter

**Correct Answer**: **A) Mother**

### Explanation:
'Only daughter of my mother' is the woman herself (since she is female).
Therefore, the man's mother is the woman herself.
So, the woman is the man's mother.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Analyze 'only daughter of my mother' -> it means 'Me'. So 'His mother is Me'. Woman is Mother. Solved in 5 seconds.

## [2023] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Ninja]
**Question 2**:
In a certain code, 'DOG' is coded as '26' and 'CAT' is coded as '24'. What is the code for 'PIG'?

**Options**:
- A) 32
- B) 42
- C) 30
- D) 36

**Correct Answer**: **A) 32**

### Explanation:
Sum of position values: P=16, I=9, G=7. Sum = 16+9+7 = 32.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Recall alphabet mappings: P(16) + I(9) + G(7) = 32. Direct sum.
