## [TCS NQT] Question 1
Pointing to a photograph, a woman says: 'His mother's only daughter is my mother.' How is the woman related to the person in the photo?
A) Daughter
B) Sister
C) Niece
D) Mother
**Answer**: `C`

### Explanation:
- 'His mother's only daughter' represents the sister of the person in the photograph.
- Since the sister of the person in the photo is 'my mother' (the speaker's mother), the speaker is the daughter of the person's sister.
- Therefore, the woman (speaker) is the Niece of the person in the photo.
