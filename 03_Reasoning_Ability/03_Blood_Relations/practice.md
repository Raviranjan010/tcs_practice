**Q11.** A is father of B. C is brother of A. D is mother of B. How is D related to C?
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `Sister-in-law`
* **Method**: Identify spouse-sibling relations.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  A is husband to D (since A is father, D is mother of B). C is brother of A. Hence, D is sister-in-law to C.
* **Target Time**: 25 seconds
</details>

## 🔴 Advanced Level (Questions 21 - 30)

**Q21.** A is father of B. C is mother of B. D is father of C. How is D related to B's brother?
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `Maternal Grandfather`
* **Method**: Draw generation gaps and identify grandparent relations.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  D is father of C, who is mother of B (and B's brother). Hence, D is maternal grandfather to B's brother.
* **Target Time**: 35 seconds
</details>
