**Q1.** Pointing to a photograph, a man says: 'His mother is my mother's only daughter.' Who is in the photo? (Variant 1)
A) His son

C) His uncle
D) Himself
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Detailed Step-by-Step Logic**: My mother's only daughter is my sister. Her son is my nephew.
