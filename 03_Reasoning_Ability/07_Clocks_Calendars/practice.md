* **Step-by-Step Solution**:

* **Answer**: `Friday`
* **Method**: Odd days = Days % 7. Shift Friday by the odd days.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  210 days / 7 leaves remainder 0. Friday + 0 days = Friday.
