## [2024] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Digital]
**Question 1**:
A pie chart shows the expense of a family. Food: 30%, Rent: 25%, Education: 20%, Savings: 15%, Others: 10%. If total income is Rs. 50000, how much is spent on Rent and Education combined?

**Options**:
- A) Rs. 22500
- B) Rs. 20000
- C) Rs. 25000
- D) Rs. 15000

**Correct Answer**: **A) Rs. 22500**

### Explanation:
Combined percentage for Rent and Education = 25% + 20% = 45%.
Total amount spent = 45% of 50000 = 0.45 * 50000 = 22500.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
45% of 50000 is half of 50000 (25000) minus 5% (25000 - 25000/10 = 22500). Quick mental math. Option A.

## [2023] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Ninja]
**Question 2**:
In a certain code, 'DOG' is coded as '26' and 'CAT' is coded as '24'. What is the code for 'PIG'?

**Options**:
- A) 32
- B) 42
- C) 30
- D) 36

**Correct Answer**: **A) 32**

### Explanation:
Sum of position values: P=16, I=9, G=7. Sum = 16+9+7 = 32.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Recall alphabet mappings: P(16) + I(9) + G(7) = 32. Direct sum.
