## 💡 Prep Strategy for TCS NQT Reasoning
1. **Draw Families**: Always draw standard symbols for gender (+/-) and generations in Blood Relations to avoid mistakes.
2. **Double Check Syllogisms**: Draw all possible overlapping cases in Venn diagrams to confirm if a negative conclusion is universally true.
3. **Circular Arrangements**: Place the first person at the bottom of the circle to make left/right movements align with your own left and right hand sides.
