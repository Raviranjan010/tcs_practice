## [TCS NQT] Question 1
6 people A, B, C, D, E, F sit in a circle facing the center.
A sits second to the right of B. C is not adjacent to A.
D sits opposite to B. E sits between D and F.
Who is seated to the immediate left of A?
A) B
B) C
C) F
D) E
**Answer**: `C`

### Explanation:
- B is placed at position 1.
- A sits second to the right of B (position 3).
- D sits opposite B (position 4).
- E sits between D and F. This leaves position 5 for E and position 6 for F.
- C sits at position 2 (since C is not adjacent to A at position 3).
- Immediate left of A (clockwise) is position 6, occupied by F.
---
