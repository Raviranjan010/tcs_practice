## [2024] [DIFFICULTY: Hard] [CATEGORY: Digital]
**Question 1**:
Six friends P, Q, R, S, T, U are sitting in a circle facing the center. R is second to the left of T. P is to the immediate right of S. U is opposite to Q. If Q is next to T, who is to the immediate left of R?

**Options**:
- A) S
- B) P
- C) U
- D) Q

**Correct Answer**: **A) S**

### Explanation:
Place T at 12 o'clock. R is second to left of T (4 o'clock, since facing inward, clockwise is left). Q is next to T (can be 10 o'clock or 2 o'clock). U is opposite Q. If Q is at 2 o'clock, U is at 8 o'clock. P is immediate right of S. Remaining slots are 6 o'clock and 10 o'clock. If S is at 6 o'clock, P is at 10 o'clock (immediate right is counter-clockwise). Thus, left of R (clockwise from 4 o'clock) is S.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Draw the circle. Left of R at 4 o'clock is 6 o'clock. Since S is at 6 o'clock, S is immediate left of R. Option A is S.

## [2023] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Ninja]
**Question 2**:
In a certain code, 'DOG' is coded as '26' and 'CAT' is coded as '24'. What is the code for 'PIG'?

**Options**:
- A) 32
- B) 42
- C) 30
- D) 36

**Correct Answer**: **A) 32**

### Explanation:
Sum of position values: P=16, I=9, G=7. Sum = 16+9+7 = 32.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Recall alphabet mappings: P(16) + I(9) + G(7) = 32. Direct sum.
