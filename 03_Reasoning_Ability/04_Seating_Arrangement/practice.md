<details>

* **Method**: Circular placement.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  B has A on his right and C on his left. Thus B sits between A and C.

## 🔴 Advanced Level (Questions 21 - 30)

**Q21.** Six people face inward in a circle. A is opposite B. C is right of A. D is between B and C. Who sits opposite D?
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `The person opposite D is the remaining unnamed slot.`
* **Method**: Visual placement of opposite slots.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  A at 6 o'clock, B at 12 o'clock. C is at 9 o'clock. D is between B and C, so D is at 10 o'clock. Opposite 10 o'clock is 4 o'clock.
* **Target Time**: 40 seconds
</details>
