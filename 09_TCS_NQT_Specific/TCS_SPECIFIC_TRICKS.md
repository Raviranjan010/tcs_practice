# 🧠 TCS NQT Specific Exam Tricks & Platform Quirks

This guide covers time management, platform quirks, coding strategies, and input/output templates.

## ⏱️ Section-by-Section Time Management

| Section | Number of Questions | Recommended Time | Time per Question |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Numerical Ability | 20 | 25 minutes | 75 seconds |
| Verbal Ability | 20 | 20 minutes | 60 seconds |
| Reasoning Ability | 20 | 25 minutes | 75 seconds |

## 📋 30 High-Probability Topics that Always Appear
- Number System (remainders, unit digits)
- Percentages & successive changes
- Profit & Loss (dishonest dealer, discounts)
- Time & Work (alternate days, efficiency)
- Time Speed Distance (trains, boats)
- Averages & Mixtures (dilution, weighted average)
- Simple & Compound Interest (installments, differences)
- Permutation & Combination (circular, grouping)
- Probability (cards, coins)
- Mensuration (cylinders, spheres)
- Logarithms (base conversions)
- Syllogisms (either-or cases)
- Coding-Decoding (ranks, shifts)
- Blood Relations (tree mapping)
- Seating Arrangement (circular facing out)
- Data Interpretation (mixed graphs)
- Clocks & Calendars (odd days, hand angles)
- Subarray Sums (Kadane's)
- Two-pointer search
- Sliding window substring search
- Binary tree level order
- Graph BFS/DFS traversal
- Dynamic programming coin change
- Matrix rotations
- String anagram and palindrome checks
- Roman to Integer conversions
- Stack balanced brackets
- Sieve of Eratosthenes
- Priority queue heap selections
- Sorting algorithms (Merge/Quick)

## ⚠️ Low ROI Topics to Skip if Short on Time
- Coordinate Geometry (distance, slope - rarely asked)
- Complex number matrices
- Height and distance trigonometry
