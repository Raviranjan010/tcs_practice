# 🎯 Interview Day: Dos and Don'ts

Follow these guidelines to make a stellar impression on the TCS interview panel.

---

## 📊 Do's & Don'ts Comparison Table

| Category | ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Preparation** | Dress in formal attire, ensure clean webcam background, keep physical copy of resume ready. | Join late, have background noise, or keep your phone un-muted. |
| **Communication** | Speak clearly, maintain eye contact with the camera, pause to think before answering. | Interrupt the interviewer or answer in simple one-word answers (e.g. just saying 'Yes' or 'No'). |
| **Technical** | Be honest. If you do not know a concept, admit it: "I am unable to recall, but I will check it after the interview." | Guess answers, make up fake projects, or bluff about skills you do not have. |
| **Behavioral** | Show enthusiasm, ask a polite question at the end (e.g. about training pathways in TCS). | Criticize your college, professors, or former employers. |
