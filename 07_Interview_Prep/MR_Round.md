# 👔 Managerial Round (MR) Prep

The Managerial Round (MR) evaluates your behavior under pressure, team collaboration skills, situational judgment, and familiarity with modern software development life cycles (Agile, Scrum).

---

## 💡 Situational Questions & Model Answers

### Q1: What would you do if a team member is not contributing to the project?
* **Model Answer**: "I would first talk to the team member privately to understand if they are facing personal issues or technical blockers. I would offer assistance or suggest dividing the tasks differently. If the issue persists and affects project delivery, I would raise it with the project manager during our sync-up, ensuring the focus remains on solving the blocker rather than blaming the individual."

### Q2: How do you handle tight deadlines or shifting requirements?
* **Model Answer**: "I prioritize tasks based on their impact using a MoSCoW matrix (Must-have, Should-have, Could-have, Won't-have). I communicate early with the stakeholders about what can be realistically delivered within the new timeline and document all changes to prevent scope creep."
