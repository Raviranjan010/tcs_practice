### Q1. Managerial situation and project leadership scenario 1?
**Interviewer Focus**: Business thinking, team dynamics, leadership qualities, agile scrum methodologies, and stress tolerance.
**Model STAR Answer**:
- **Situation**: During our final year project, a team member left 1 week before the submission deadline.
- **Task**: We had to finish the pending database migration module.
- **Action**: I re-allocated the tasks, took on the database migration myself, and worked extra hours.
- **Result**: We submitted the project on time and scored an A grade.
**Key Advice**: Show that you remain calm under pressure and are a team player.
