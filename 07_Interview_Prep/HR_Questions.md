# 🤝 HR Round Questions

The HR Round checks if you are culturally fit for TCS, verifying your communication skills, long-term commitment, shift flexibility, and documentation correctness.

---

## 💡 Top HR Questions & Model Answers

### Q1: Why do you want to join TCS?
* **Model Answer**: "TCS is a pioneer in the IT services sector and offers an excellent learning curve for freshers through its structured training programs (like Initial Learning Program - ILP). I am excited about the opportunity to work on diverse global projects and grow my career within a stable and employee-friendly organization."

### Q2: Are you willing to relocate and work in night shifts?
* **Model Answer**: "Yes, absolutely. I am fully flexible to relocate to any TCS office location based on project requirements. I am also comfortable working in rotational night shifts, as I understand that IT operations require 24/7 support for global clients."

### Q3: Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
* **Model Answer**: "In 5 years, I see myself growing into a senior technical lead role, where I can architect software systems and mentor junior developers. I also plan to acquire certifications in cloud computing and project management to contribute to TCS's digital transformation initiatives."
