# 🗺️ 60-Day TCS NQT Preparation Roadmap

This roadmap is structured to take you from a complete beginner to exam-ready for the **Prime/Digital** levels in 60 days. Follow the daily tasks, read the notes, study the PYQs, and solve the practice problems.

---

## 📅 Roadmap Overview

```
|--- Phase 1 (Days 1–18)  ---|--- Phase 2 (Days 19–30) ---|--- Phase 3 (Days 31–40) ---|--- Phase 4 (Days 41–55) ---|--- Phase 5 (Days 56–60) ---|
|  Numerical Ability & Quant |       Verbal Ability       |      Reasoning Ability     |   Coding & Prog. Logic     |    Mocks, Interview & Rev. |
```

---

## 🛠️ Phase-by-Phase Day Plan

### 🔢 Phase 1: Numerical Ability & Advanced Quant (Days 1 – 18)
*Focus: Master core mathematical concepts, shortcut tricks, and speed arithmetic.*

* **Day 1**: [Number System](01_Numerical_Ability/01_Number_System/) (LCM, GCD, Divisibility Rules, Unit Digits)
* **Day 2**: [Percentages](01_Numerical_Ability/02_Percentages/) (Fraction to percentage conversions, successive percentage change)
* **Day 3**: [Profit & Loss](01_Numerical_Ability/03_Profit_Loss/) (Discount, marked price, cheat seller concepts)
* **Day 4**: [Time & Work / Pipes & Cisterns](01_Numerical_Ability/04_Time_Work_Pipes/) (Efficiency method, LCM method)
* **Day 5**: [Time, Speed & Distance](01_Numerical_Ability/05_Time_Speed_Distance/) (Average speed, relative speed, trains, boats)
* **Day 6**: [Averages, Mixtures & Alligations](01_Numerical_Ability/06_Averages_Mixtures/) (Weighted average, replacement formulas)
* **Day 7**: [Ratio, Proportion & Partnerships](01_Numerical_Ability/07_Ratio_Proportion/) (Dividing ratios, mean proportional)
* **Day 8**: [Simple & Compound Interest](01_Numerical_Ability/08_Simple_Compound_Interest/) (CI-SI difference formulas, compounding periods)
* **Day 9**: [Probability](01_Numerical_Ability/09_Probability/) (Coins, dice, cards, balls in bags, conditional probability)
* **Day 10**: [Permutation & Combination](01_Numerical_Ability/10_Permutation_Combination/) (Circular arrangement, word formation, combinations)
* **Day 11**: [Geometry & Mensuration](01_Numerical_Ability/11_Geometry_Mensuration/) (Area & volume of 2D/3D shapes, standard theorems)
* **Day 12**: [Logarithms](01_Numerical_Ability/12_Logarithms/) (Log properties, change of base, characteristic & mantissa)
* **Day 13**: [Advanced Number Theory](06_Advanced_Quantitative/Advanced_Number_Theory.md) (Euler's totient, Fermat's Little Theorem, remainder theorems)
* **Day 14**: [Advanced Probability](06_Advanced_Quantitative/Advanced_Probability.md) (Bayes' Theorem, probability distributions)
* **Day 15**: [Advanced DI](06_Advanced_Quantitative/Advanced_DI.md) (Radar graphs, mixed charts, missing data charts)
* **Day 16-18**: Revision of all quant topics using the [Quant Cheat Sheet](01_Numerical_Ability/CHEAT_SHEET.md) and practice of hard-level questions.

## 💡 Daily Study Routine (Recommended: 4 Hours)
1. **Concept Study (1 Hour)**: Study the `notes.md` file of the day's topic. Understand the formulas and Vedic math shortcuts.
2. **PYQ Review (1 Hour)**: Review the past year questions in `pyq.md`. Practice solving them under 60 seconds.
3. **Practice Exercises (1 Hour)**: Solve the 30+ graded questions in `practice.md`. Check answers using the details collapsible tags.
4. **Hands-on Coding (1 Hour)**: Implement at least 2 coding problems from [05_Coding_Section](05_Coding_Section/README.md) using C++. Pay attention to time and space complexities.
