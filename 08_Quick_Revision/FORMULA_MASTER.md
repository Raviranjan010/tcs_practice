## ⏱️ SECTION C: COMPLEXITY CHEAT SHEET
- Binary Search: $O(\log N)$ time, $O(1)$ space.
- Merge Sort: $O(N \log N)$ time, $O(N)$ space.
- Quick Sort: $O(N \log N)$ average time, $O(N^2)$ worst-time, $O(\log N)$ space.
- Bubble Sort / Insertion Sort: $O(N^2)$ time, $O(1)$ space.
- Hash Map Access: $O(1)$ average time, $O(N)$ worst-time.
- Binary Tree BFS/DFS: $O(N)$ time, $O(H)$ space.
- Dijkstra's Algorithm: $O((V+E)\log V)$ time.
- Floyd-Warshall: $O(V^3)$ time, $O(V^2)$ space.
