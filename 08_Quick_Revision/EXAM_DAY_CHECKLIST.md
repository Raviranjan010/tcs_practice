# 📋 TCS NQT Exam Day Checklist

Make sure you check off every item on this list before your NQT session starts.

---

## 🏢 In-Center Exam Checklist
* [ ] **Admit Card**: Printed copy of your Hall Ticket with your photograph pasted.
* [ ] **Identity Proof**: Original government ID (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Passport) and college ID.
* [ ] **Stationery**: Clear transparent pen. Rough paper sheets will be provided at the center.
* [ ] **Time**: Reach the exam center at least 60 minutes before the gate closing time.

---

## 🏠 Remote (Home-Based) Exam Checklist
* [ ] **Webcam & Mic**: Functioning web camera and microphone checked via system diagnostic tool.
* [ ] **Internet**: Stable broadband connection (minimum 2 Mbps speed) with a mobile hotspot backup.
* [ ] **Rough Work**: Keep exactly one blank sheet of A4 paper and a pen. Show both sides to the webcam if prompted.
* [ ] **Room Lighting**: Ensure the light source is in front of you, not behind. Your face must be clearly lit.
* [ ] **Background**: Quiet room with no other person present. Lock the door to prevent interruptions.
