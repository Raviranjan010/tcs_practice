**Q21.** What is the worst-case space complexity of recursive Merge Sort?
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `O(N)`
* **Method**: Merge sort auxiliary space analysis.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Merge sort requires N auxiliary array space to merge elements, and O(log N) stack frames. Total is O(N).
* **Target Time**: 25 seconds
</details>
