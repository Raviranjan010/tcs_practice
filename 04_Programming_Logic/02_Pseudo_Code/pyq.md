## [TCS NQT] Question 1
Evaluate: Integer x=5. x = x << 1. print x. (Variant 1)

A) 10
B) 5
C) 2
D) 15
**Answer**: `A`

### Explanation:
x << 1 multiplies x by 2. 5 * 2 = 10.

## [TCS NQT] Question 2
Evaluate: Integer a=8, b=12. a = a & b. b = b | a. print a+b. (Variant 2)

A) 20
B) 16
C) 12
D) 8
**Answer**: `A`

### Explanation:
a = 8 & 12 = 8. b = 12 | 8 = 12. Sum = 8 + 12 = 20.
