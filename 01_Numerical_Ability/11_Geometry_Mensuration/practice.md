**Q1.** Find the area of a square whose side is 4 cm.
A) A) 16 sq.cm
B) B) 21 sq.cm
C) C) 13 sq.cm
D) D) 26 sq.cm
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Area of Square Formula
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Area of square = side * side.
  Side = 4 cm.
  Area = 4 * 4 = 16 sq.cm.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
