**Q21.** Three spheres of radii 6cm, 8cm, and 10cm are melted to form a single sphere. Find its radius.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `12.0`
* **Method**: Volume sum: $R = \sqrt[3]{r1^3 + r2^3 + r3^3}$.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  R^3 = 6^3 + 8^3 + 10^3 = 1728. R = \sqrt[3]{1728} = 12.0 cm.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
