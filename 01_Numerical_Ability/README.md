## 💡 Prep Strategy for TCS NQT Quant
1. **Focus on Speed**: You have roughly 75 seconds per question in the Foundation section and 120 seconds in the Advanced section. Memorize fraction-to-percentage conversions.
2. **Master Divisibility**: Many remainder and number system questions can be solved in <30 seconds by checking divisibility rules.
3. **Use Alligation**: It is a powerful method applicable to averages, profit/loss, simple interest, and ratio problems.
