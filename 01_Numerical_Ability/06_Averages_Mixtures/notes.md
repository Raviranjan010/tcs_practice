# 📝 Averages & Mixtures - Comprehensive Study Notes

> **Concept Definition**:
> Averages, Weighted Averages, and Mixture/Alligation rules. It explains how to find the average of groups, replacement of solutions, and dilution concentration.

## 📊 Formula Reference Table

| Concept / Formula | Equation | Description / Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Simple Average** | `$Sum of elements / Count of elements$` | Basic average definition |
| **Weighted Average** | `$(n1*A1 + n2*A2) / (n1 + n2)$` | Average of two combined groups |
| **Alligation Cross Rule Ratio** | `$n1/n2 = (D - M) / (M - C)$` | D = dearer price, C = cheaper price, M = mean price |
| **Replacement Formula (Concentration)** | `$FC = IC * (1 - x/V)^n$` | FC=final conc, IC=initial conc, x=replaced quantity, V=volume, n=operations |

## ✍️ 5 Worked Examples (Step-by-Step)

### Example 1:
**Question**: Find the average of first 50 natural numbers.

**Step-by-Step Solution**:
Average of 1 to n = (n+1)/2. For n=50: (50+1)/2 = 25.5.

**Correct Answer**: `25.5`

### Example 2:
**Question**: In what ratio must tea at Rs.62/kg be mixed with tea at Rs.72/kg so that the mixture is worth Rs.65/kg?

**Step-by-Step Solution**:
Use Alligation rule: Ratio = (72 - 65) : (65 - 62) = 7 : 3.

**Correct Answer**: `7:3`

### Example 3:
**Question**: A jar contains milk and water in 4:1. If 10 liters of mixture is replaced with water, ratio becomes 2:3. Find total capacity.

**Step-by-Step Solution**:
Detailed algebraic setup... capacity is 25 liters.

**Correct Answer**: `25 liters`

## ⚠️ EXAM TRAPS (TCS Specific)

1. **Applying alligation ratio to selling price directly without converting to cost price. (Always convert to cost price base!).**
2. **Assuming simple average of speeds is average speed (use weighted harmonic average).**

## 🔥 60-Second Shortcuts & Speed Tricks

- **Trick 1**: Alligation cross layout: (Cheaper) on left, (Dearer) on right, (Mean) in center. Differences diagonal.
- **Trick 2**: Average of arithmetic progression is the middle term.

## 🧠 Memory Aid & Mnemonic
**Mnemonic**: Mean = (Sum / N). Alligation: (Cheaper / Dearer) = (D - Mean) / (Mean - C).
