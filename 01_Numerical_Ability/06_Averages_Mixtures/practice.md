**Q1.** Find the average of two numbers: 15 and 35.
A) A) 25.0
B) B) 26.0
C) C) 24.0
D) D) 27.0
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Simple Average
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Sum of terms = 15 + 35 = 50.
  Average = Sum / Count = 50 / 2 = 25.0.
