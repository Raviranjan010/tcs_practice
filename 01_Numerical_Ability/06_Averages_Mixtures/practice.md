**Q11.** The average age of 5 employees is 21 years. If a new employee of age 33 joins, find the new average age.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `23.0`
* **Method**: New Average = (Sum + New Age) / (Count + 1).
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Sum = 105. New Sum = 105 + 33 = 138. New Average = 138 / 6 = 23.0 years.
* **Target Time**: 35 seconds
</details>

## 🔴 Advanced Level (Questions 21 - 30)

**Q21.** A vessel contains 40 liters of milk. 6 liters of milk is replaced with water. This process is repeated 3 times. Find the remaining milk.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `24.56`
* **Method**: Formula: $FC = IC \left(1 - \frac{rep}{V}\right)^3$.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Milk remaining = 40 * (1 - 6/40)^3 = 24.56 liters.
* **Target Time**: 50 seconds
</details>
