## [TCS NQT] [2023] [Ninja] [Medium] Question 1

The average weight of 8 persons increases by 2.5 kg when a new person comes in place of one of them weighing 65 kg. What is the weight of the new person?

A) 70 kg
B) 85 kg
C) 80 kg
D) 75 kg

**Answer**: `B`

### Explanation:
1. Total weight increase = 8 * 2.5 = 20 kg.
2. Weight of new person = Weight of replaced person + increase = 65 + 20 = 85 kg.

## [TCS NQT] [2023] [Ninja] [Medium] Question 3

A vessel contains 60 liters of milk. 6 liters of milk is taken out and replaced with water. This process is repeated two more times. Find the amount of milk left in the vessel.

A) 43.74 liters
B) 48.6 liters
C) 54 liters
D) 40 liters

**Answer**: `A`

### Explanation:
1. Use replacement formula: Milk left = 60 * (1 - 6/60)^3.
2. Left = 60 * (0.9)^3 = 60 * 0.729 = 43.74 liters.
