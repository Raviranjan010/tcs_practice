**Q11.** How many diagonals can be drawn in a polygon of 4 sides?
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `2`
* **Method**: Use formula $n(n-3)/2$.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Diagonals = 4 * (4 - 3) / 2 = 2.
* **Target Time**: 20 seconds
</details>

* **Method**: Circular permutation: (n-1)!
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Circular arrangements = (6 - 1)! = 5! = 120.
