**Q1.** In how many ways can 5 distinct books be arranged on a single shelf?
A) A) 120
B) B) 130
C) C) 110
D) D) 140
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Factorial Permutation
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  The number of ways to arrange n distinct objects in a row is given by n!.
  Here, n = 5. Arrangements = 5! = 120.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
