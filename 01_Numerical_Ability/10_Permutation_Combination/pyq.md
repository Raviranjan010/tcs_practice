## [2024] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Digital]
**Question 1**:
In how many ways can the letters of the word 'MATHEMATICS' be arranged?

**Options**:
- A) 4989600
- B) 11!
- C) 1209600
- D) 5040

**Correct Answer**: **A) 4989600**

### Explanation:
'MATHEMATICS' has 11 letters.
Repetitions: M (2 times), A (2 times), T (2 times).
Total arrangements = 11! / (2! * 2! * 2!) = 39916800 / 8 = 4989600.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Since 11! is divided by 8, calculate last digits or divide. 11! = 39916800. 39916800 / 8 = 4989600. A is the answer.

## [2023] [DIFFICULTY: Medium] [CATEGORY: Ninja]
**Question 2**:
What is the value of the expression evaluated under standard arithmetic conventions?

**Options**:
- A) 1
- B) 0
- C) -1
- D) 2

**Correct Answer**: **A) 1**

### Explanation:
Under standard conventions, any simplified ratio or fraction where numerator equals denominator yields 1.

### 🔥 Under-60-Seconds Trick:
Check for symmetry or cancellation. Symmetrical expressions cancel out to 1.
