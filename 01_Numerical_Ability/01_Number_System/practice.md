## 🟢 Beginner Level (Questions 1 - 10)

**Q1.** Find the remainder when $2^{11}$ is divided by 5.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `3`
* **Method**: Using modular arithmetic and Euler's totient or cycle patterns.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Euler totient of 5 is 4. Power 11 mod 4 = 3. So $2^{11} \equiv 2^{3} \equiv 3 \pmod5$.
* **Target Time**: 30 seconds
</details>

## 🔴 Advanced Level (Questions 21 - 30)

**Q21.** Find the remainder when $7^{60}$ is divided by 25.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `1`
* **Method**: Use Euler's totient theorem. $\phi(25) = 20$. So $7^{20} \equiv 1 \pmod{25}$.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  $\phi(25) = 25*(1 - 1/5) = 20$. Divide power 60 by 20. Remainder is 0. So $7^{60} \equiv 7^{0} \equiv 1 \pmod25$.
* **Target Time**: 50 seconds
</details>
