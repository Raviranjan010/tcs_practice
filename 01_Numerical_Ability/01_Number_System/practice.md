## 🟢 Beginner Level (Questions 1 - 15)

**Q1.** When the positive integer N = 20 is divided by 11, what is the remainder?
A) A) 9
B) B) 10
C) C) 0
D) D) 1
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Remainder Division
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  We are asked to find N mod 11 where N = 20.
  Dividing 20 by 11 gives quotient 1 and remainder 9.
  Therefore, remainder is 9.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
