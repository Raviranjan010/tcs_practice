## 🟢 Beginner Level (Questions 1 - 15)

**Q1.** A train covers a distance of 180 km in 3 hours. Calculate its speed in km/h.
A) A) 60 km/h
B) B) 70 km/h
C) C) 55 km/h
D) D) 75 km/h
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Speed Formula
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Speed = Distance / Time.
  Distance = 180 km, Time = 3 hours.
  Speed = 180 / 3 = 60 km/h.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
