**Q11.** A boat travels downstream a distance of 220 km in 2 hours and upstream 210 km in 2 hours. Find the speed of the stream.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `2.5`
* **Method**: Find downstream and upstream speeds, then Stream = (Downstream - Upstream)/2.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Downstream speed = 110.0 km/h. Upstream speed = 105.0 km/h. Stream = (110.0 - 105.0)/2 = 2.5 km/h.
* **Target Time**: 40 seconds
</details>

**Q21.** A man covers a distance traveling at 40 km/h. If he had traveled at 50 km/h, he would have arrived 6 minutes early. Find the distance.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `20.0`
* **Method**: Distance = (Product of speeds / Difference of speeds) * Time difference (in hours).
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Distance = (40 * 50 / 10) * (6 / 60) = 20.0 km.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
