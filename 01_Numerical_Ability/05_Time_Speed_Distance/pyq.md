## [TCS NQT] [2023] [Ninja] [Medium] Question 1

A train 240 m long passes a pole in 24 seconds. How long will it take to pass a platform 650 m long?

A) 89 seconds
B) 65 seconds
C) 100 seconds
D) 150 seconds

**Answer**: `A`

### Explanation:
1. Speed of train = Length / Time = 240 / 24 = 10 m/s.
2. To pass the platform, distance = Train length + Platform length = 240 + 650 = 890 m.
3. Time taken = 890 / 10 = 89 seconds.

A) 2 km
B) 1 km
C) 1.5 km
D) 0.5 km

**Answer**: `A`

### Explanation:
1. Relative speed = 11 - 10 = 1 km/h = 1 * (5/18) = 5/18 m/s.
2. Time to catch thief = Distance / Relative speed = 200 / (5/18) = 200 * 18 / 5 = 720 seconds.
3. Distance run by thief = Speed of thief * Time = 10 km/h * (720 / 3600 hours) = 10 * 0.2 = 2 km.

A motorboat whose speed is 15 km/h in still water goes 30 km downstream and comes back in a total of 4 hours 30 minutes. The speed of the stream is:

A) 5 km/h
B) 4 km/h
C) 6 km/h
D) 3 km/h

**Answer**: `A`

### Explanation:
1. Let stream speed be r. Total time = 4.5 hours.
2. Equation: 30 / (15 + r) + 30 / (15 - r) = 4.5.
3. Simplify: 30 * [ (15-r + 15+r) / (225 - r^2) ] = 4.5 => 30 * 30 / (225 - r^2) = 4.5.
4. 900 = 4.5 * (225 - r^2) => 200 = 225 - r^2 => r^2 = 25 => r = 5 km/h.
