**Q11.** Find the Compound Interest on Rs. 11000 for 2 years at 10% p.a. compounded annually.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `2310.0`
* **Method**: CI = P * ((1 + R/100)^T - 1).
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Amount = 11000 * (1.1)^2 = 11000 * 1.21 = 13310.0. CI = 13310.0 - 11000 = 2310.0.
* **Target Time**: 40 seconds
</details>
