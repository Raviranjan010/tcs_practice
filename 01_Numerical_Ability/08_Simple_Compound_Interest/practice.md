## 🟢 Beginner Level (Questions 1 - 15)

**Q1.** Find the simple interest on Principal Rs. 2000 at interest rate of 5% per annum for 2 years.
A) A) Rs. 200
B) B) Rs. 210
C) C) Rs. 190
D) D) Rs. 220
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Simple Interest Formula
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Simple Interest (SI) = (P * R * T) / 100.
  SI = (2000 * 5 * 2) / 100 = 200.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
