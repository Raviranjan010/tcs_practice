**Q1.** Find the value of log_2(8).
A) A) 3
B) B) 4
C) C) 2
D) D) 5
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Logarithm Definition
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Let log_2(8) = x. This means 2^x = 8.
  Since 2^3 = 8, x must be equal to 3.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
