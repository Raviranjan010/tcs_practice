## 🟡 Intermediate Level (Questions 11 - 20)

**Q11.** Simplify $\log_{10} 110 - \log_{10} 11$.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `1`
* **Method**: Use quotient rule: $\log(A) - \log(B) = \log(A/B)$.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  $\log_{10} (110 / 11) = \log_{10} 10 = 1$.
* **Target Time**: 20 seconds
</details>

## 🔴 Advanced Level (Questions 21 - 30)

**Q21.** Find the number of digits in $3^{21}$ (given $\log_{10} 3 = 0.4771$).
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `11`
* **Method**: Digits = $\lfloor N \log_{10} 3 \rfloor + 1$.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  $\log_{10} 3^{21} = 21 \times 0.4771 = 10.0191$. Digits = \lfloor 10.0191 \rfloor + 1 = 11.
* **Target Time**: 40 seconds
</details>
