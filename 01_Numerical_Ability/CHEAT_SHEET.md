## 💰 Complete Simple and Compound Interest Formulas

| Name of Formula | Equation | Parameter Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Simple Interest** | $SI = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100}$ | P = Principal, R = Annual Rate, T = Time in years |
| **Compound Amount** | $A = P \left(1 + \frac{R}{100}\right)^n$ | n = number of annual compounding periods |
| **CI-SI 2 Years Diff** | $Diff = P \left(\frac{R}{100}\right)^2$ | Difference between CI and SI for 2 years |
| **CI-SI 3 Years Diff** | $Diff = P \left(\frac{R}{100}\right)^2 \left(\frac{300 + R}{100}\right)$ | Difference between CI and SI for 3 years |
| **Half-Yearly CI** | $A = P \left(1 + \frac{R}{200}\right)^{2n}$ | R is annual rate, n is years |
| **Quarterly CI** | $A = P \left(1 + \frac{R}{400}\right)^{4n}$ | R is annual rate, n is years |

## 🎲 Permutation and Combination Formulas

- **nPr (Arrangements)**: $nPr = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}$
- **nCr (Selections)**: $nCr = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}$
- **Circular Permutations (distinct)**: $P_{circ} = (n-1)!$
- **Circular Permutations (necklaces)**: $P_{neck} = \frac{(n-1)!}{2}$
- **Arrangements with identical items**: $\frac{n!}{a! \times b! \times c!}$ where a, b, c are repeat frequencies
- **Number of diagonals in an n-sided polygon**: $\frac{n(n-3)}{2}$

## 📈 Logarithm Rules & Standard Base Values

- **Product Rule**: $\log_b(xy) = \log_b(x) + \log_b(y)$
- **Quotient Rule**: $\log_b(x/y) = \log_b(x) - \log_b(y)$
- **Power Rule**: $\log_b(x^k) = k \log_b(x)$
- **Base Change**: $\log_b(a) = \frac{\log_c(a)}{\log_c(b)}$

## 🧠 Vedic Mathematics Quick Speed Hacks

### 1. Multiplication near base 100
Formula: $(100 - a) \times (100 - b) = 10000 - 100(a+b) + ab$
*Example*: $98 \times 97$ (a = 2, b = 3):
- Subtract a+b from 100: $100 - (2+3) = 95$. (This forms the first two digits).
- Multiply the deficits: $2 \times 3 = 06$. (This forms the last two digits).
- Result = **9506**.

### 2. Percentage speed approximations
- **1%**: Shift decimal point left by 2 positions (e.g. 1% of 340 is 3.4).
- **10%**: Shift decimal point left by 1 position (e.g. 10% of 340 is 34).
- **12.5%**: Divide by 8 (since $12.5\% = \frac{1}{8}$).
- **25%**: Divide by 4 (since $25\% = \frac{1}{4}$).
- **33.33%**: Divide by 3 (since $33.33\% = \frac{1}{3}$).
- **50%**: Divide by 2.
- **75%**: Divide by 4 and multiply by 3.
