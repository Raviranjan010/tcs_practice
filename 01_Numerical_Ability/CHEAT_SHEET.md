## ⚡ Divisibility Rules (2 to 19)

* **2**: Last digit is even.
* **3**: Sum of digits is divisible by 3.
* **4**: Last two digits are divisible by 4.
* **5**: Last digit is 0 or 5.
* **6**: Divisible by both 2 and 3.
* **7**: Double the last digit and subtract it from the remaining number. Result is divisible by 7. (e.g., 343 -> 34 - 2*3 = 28, divisible by 7).
* **8**: Last three digits are divisible by 8.
* **9**: Sum of digits is divisible by 9.
* **10**: Last digit is 0.
* **11**: Difference between sum of digits at odd places and even places is 0 or a multiple of 11.
* **12**: Divisible by both 3 and 4.
* **13**: Multiply the last digit by 4 and add it to the remaining number. Repeat. (e.g., 143 -> 14 + 3*4 = 26, divisible by 13).
* **14**: Divisible by both 2 and 7.
* **15**: Divisible by both 3 and 5.
* **16**: Last four digits are divisible by 16.
* **17**: Multiply the last digit by 5 and subtract it from the remaining number.
* **18**: Even number and divisible by 9.
* **19**: Multiply the last digit by 2 and add it to the remaining number.

## 🧠 Vedic Math Tricks for NQT

### 1. Multiplication by 11
Add adjacent digits from right to left.
* *Example*: $2435 	imes 11$:
  - Write down the last digit: 5
  - Add $5 + 3 = 8$
  - Add $3 + 4 = 7$
  - Add $4 + 2 = 6$

### 2. Squaring a number ending in 5
Multiply the non-5 digits by the next consecutive integer, then append "25" at the end.
* *Example*: $75^2$:
  - Non-5 digit: 7. Next integer: $7 + 1 = 8$.
  - Multiply: $7 	imes 8 = 56$.
  - Append 25: **5625**.
