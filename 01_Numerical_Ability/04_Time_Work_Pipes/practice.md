## 🟢 Beginner Level (Questions 1 - 15)

**Q1.** A can complete a piece of work in 3 days, and B can complete it in 6 days. Working together, in how many days will they complete the work?
A) A) 2.0 days
B) B) 3.0 days
C) C) 1.5 days
D) D) 4.0 days
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: LCM / Combined Rate Method
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  A's daily work rate = 1/3.
  B's daily work rate = 1/6.
  Combined daily rate = 1/3 + 1/6 = (6+3)/(3*6).
  Time taken together = (3*6)/(3+6) = 2.0 days.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
