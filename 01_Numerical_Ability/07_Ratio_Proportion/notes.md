# 📝 Ratio & Proportion - Comprehensive Study Notes

> **Concept Definition**:
> Ratios, compound ratios, proportions (mean, third, fourth), and partnerships where profit is shared in proportion to capital * time.

## 📊 Formula Reference Table

| Concept / Formula | Equation | Description / Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Compounded Ratio** | `$(a*c) : (b*d)$` | Compound of a:b and c:d |
| **Mean Proportion of a and b** | `$sqrt(a*b)$` | Mean proportional |
| **Third Proportional of a and b** | `$b^2 / a$` | Derived from a:b :: b:c |
| **Fourth Proportional of a, b, c** | `$b*c / a$` | Derived from a:b :: c:d |
| **Partnership Profit Ratio** | `$P1:P2 = C1*T1 : C2*T2$` | C = capital, T = time |

## ✍️ 5 Worked Examples (Step-by-Step)

### Example 1:
**Question**: If A:B = 2:3 and B:C = 4:5, find A:B:C.

**Step-by-Step Solution**:
Multiply A:B by 4 and B:C by 3: A:B = 8:12, B:C = 12:15. A:B:C = 8:12:15.

**Correct Answer**: `8:12:15`

### Example 2:
**Question**: Find mean proportional of 9 and 16.

**Step-by-Step Solution**:
Mean proportion = sqrt(9 * 16) = sqrt(144) = 12.

**Correct Answer**: `12`

### Example 3:
**Question**: A and B invest Rs.20000 and Rs.30000 in business. A leaves after 6 months. If annual profit is Rs.20000, find A's share.

**Step-by-Step Solution**:
Ratio = (20000 * 6) : (30000 * 12) = 120000 : 360000 = 1 : 3. A's share = 1/4 * 20000 = Rs.5000.

**Correct Answer**: `Rs. 5000`

## ⚠️ EXAM TRAPS (TCS Specific)

1. **Adding ratio values directly without common scale factor.**
2. **Confusing direct proportion (y=kx) with inverse proportion (y=k/x).**

## 🔥 60-Second Shortcuts & Speed Tricks

- **Trick 1**: Write A, B, C side-by-side, fill empty spaces with adjacent numbers, and multiply columns.

## 🧠 Memory Aid & Mnemonic
**Mnemonic**: Mean Proportional = sqrt(ab). Third Proportional = b^2/a.
