# 📝 Ratio & Proportion - Concept & Shortcuts

> **Concept Definition**:
> Ratio compares two quantities of the same kind by division. Proportion states that two ratios are equal. Partnership deals with business ventures where partners invest capital for specific time periods.

## 📊 Formula / Trick Table

| Concept / Formula | Mathematical Expression |
| :--- | :--- |
| Fourth Proportional to a, b, c | $x = \frac{bc}{a}$ |
| Third Proportional to a, b | $x = \frac{b^2}{a}$ |
| Mean Proportional between a, b | $x = \sqrt{ab}$ |
| Partnership Investment Rule | $\frac{\text{Profit}_A}{\text{Profit}_B} = \frac{C_A \times T_A}{C_B \times T_B}$ where $C$ is capital, $T$ is time. |

## ✍️ Worked Examples (Step-by-Step)

### Example 1:
**Question**: If A:B = 2:3 and B:C = 4:5, find A:B:C.

**Solution**:
Step 1: To merge ratios, equalize B. B is 3 in A:B, and 4 in B:C. LCM of 3 and 4 is 12.
Step 2: Multiply A:B by 4: $A:B = 8:12$.
Step 3: Multiply B:C by 3: $B:C = 12:15$.
Step 4: Merge them: $A:B:C = 8:12:15$.

### Example 2:
**Question**: Find the mean proportional between 9 and 16.

**Solution**:
Step 1: Mean Proportional formula is $\sqrt{ab}$.
Step 2: $x = \sqrt{9 \times 16} = \sqrt{144}$.
Step 3: $x = 12$.
Step 4: The mean proportional is 12.

### Example 3:
**Question**: A and B invest in a business in the ratio 3:2. If 5% of the total profit goes to charity and A's share is Rs. 855, find the total profit.

**Solution**:
Step 1: Let the total profit be $P$. Net profit for distribution = $0.95P$.
Step 2: A's share of profit = $\frac{3}{3+2} \times 0.95P = \frac{3}{5} \times 0.95P = 0.57P$.
Step 3: Given $0.57P = 855 \implies P = 855 / 0.57 = 1500$.
Step 4: The total profit is Rs. 1500.

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ **Pitfall**: Students often divide profit based only on capital invested, forgetting that the time duration of investment must be multiplied by the capital if the time durations are different.

## 💡 Shortcut / Vedic Trick

🔥 **Vedic Shortcut**: To balance A:B:C quickly, write them side-by-side and fill the empty spaces with the adjacent number. Multiply vertically. (e.g. A B C: 2 3 [3] / [4] 4 5 => 8 12 15).
