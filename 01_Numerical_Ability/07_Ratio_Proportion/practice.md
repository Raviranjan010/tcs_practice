* **Method**: A/C = (A/B) * (B/C).

**Q11.** If A:B = 11:12 and B:C = 12:13, find the merged ratio A:B:C.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `11:12:13`
* **Method**: Balance the common term B.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Since B is already balanced (12 in both ratios), the merged ratio is simply 11:12:13.
* **Target Time**: 15 seconds

## 🔴 Advanced Level (Questions 21 - 30)

**Q21.** Three numbers are in the ratio 3:4:5. The sum of their squares is 2100. Find the numbers.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `19.4, 25.9, 32.4`
* **Method**: Let numbers be $3x, 4x, 5x$. Solve $(3x)^2 + (4x)^2 + (5x)^2 = {i * 100}$.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  50x^2 = 2100 \implies x^2 = 42 \implies x = 6.48. Numbers are 3x, 4x, 5x.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
