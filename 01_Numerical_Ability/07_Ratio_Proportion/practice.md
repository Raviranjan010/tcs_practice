## 🟢 Beginner Level (Questions 1 - 15)

**Q1.** In a partnership, A invests Rs. 2000 and B invests Rs. 6000. Find A's share ratio in the total profit.
A) A) 1:4
B) B) 1:3
C) C) 2:5
D) D) 3:4
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Investment Ratio
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Ratio of investment = A:B = 2000 : 6000 = 2:6 = 1:3.
  Total parts = 1 + 3 = 4. A's share ratio is 1 out of 4, i.e., 1:4.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>

B) B) 1:3
C) C) 2:5
D) D) 3:4
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Investment Ratio
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Ratio of investment = A:B = 44000 : 132000 = 44:132 = 1:3.
  Total parts = 1 + 3 = 4. A's share ratio is 1 out of 4, i.e., 1:4.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>
