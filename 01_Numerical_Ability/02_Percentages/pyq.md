## [TCS NQT] [2023] [Ninja] [Medium] Question 1

A shopkeeper marks his goods 40% above cost. He then gives a 10% discount. Find his net profit percentage.

A) 30%
B) 26%
C) 28%
D) 25%

**Answer**: `B`

### Explanation:
1. Let cost price (CP) = 100.
2. Marked price (MP) = 40% above CP = 140.
3. Discount = 10% of 140 = 14.
4. Selling price (SP) = 140 - 14 = 126.
5. Profit % = SP - CP = 126 - 100 = 26%.
Alternatively, use successive change formula: a = 40, b = -10. Net change = 40 - 10 + (40 * -10)/100 = 30 - 4 = 26%.

## [TCS NQT] [2023] [Ninja] [Medium] Question 3

In an examination, 35% of students failed in Hindi, 45% failed in English, and 20% failed in both. Find the percentage of students who passed in both subjects.

A) 40%
B) 60%
C) 80%
D) 20%

**Answer**: `A`

### Explanation:
1. Let fail in Hindi = A (35%), fail in English = B (45%), fail in both = A intersect B (20%).
2. Total failed in at least one = P(A) + P(B) - P(A intersect B) = 35 + 45 - 20 = 60%.
3. Percentage of students who passed in both = 100% - 60% = 40%.
