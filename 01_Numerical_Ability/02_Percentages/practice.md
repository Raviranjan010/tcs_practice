**Q1.** Calculate 15% of the value 300.
A) A) 45
B) B) 50
C) C) 40
D) D) 55
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Percentage Calculation
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Formula: (Percentage / 100) * Value.
