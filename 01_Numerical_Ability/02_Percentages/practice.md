**Q11.** If A's income is first increased by 20% and then decreased by 10%, find the net percentage change in income.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `8.0%`
* **Method**: Successive percentage change formula $a + b + ab/100$.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Net = 20 - 10 - (20*10)/100 = 20 - 10 - 2 = 8% increase.
* **Target Time**: 30 seconds
</details>

**Q21.** In an election, A and B contested. 10% voters did not vote and 10% votes were invalid. If A got 42% of valid votes and won by 1620 votes, find total voters.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `9999`
* **Method**: Set up equation. Let total voters = X.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Valid votes = 81% of X. Winner's margin = 20% of valid votes. $0.81X \times 20/100 = 1620 \implies X = 9999$.
* **Target Time**: 60 seconds
</details>
