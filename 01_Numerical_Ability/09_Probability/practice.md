## 🟢 Beginner Level (Questions 1 - 15)

**Q1.** A bag contains 10 balls, of which 2 are red. If one ball is drawn at random, what is the probability that it is red?
A) A) 0.2
B) B) 0.30000000000000004
C) C) 0.1
D) D) 0.4
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Classical Probability Definition
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Probability = Favorable Outcomes / Total Outcomes.
  Favorable (red balls) = 2, Total = 10.
  Probability = 2 / 10 = 0.2.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>

**Q45.** A bag contains 10 balls, of which 1 are red. If one ball is drawn at random, what is the probability that it is red?
A) A) 0.1
B) B) 0.2
C) C) 0.0
D) D) 0.30000000000000004
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Classical Probability Definition
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Probability = Favorable Outcomes / Total Outcomes.
  Favorable (red balls) = 1, Total = 10.
  Probability = 1 / 10 = 0.1.
