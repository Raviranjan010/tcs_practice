**Q21.** Two cards are drawn together from a pack of 52 cards. What is the probability that one is a club and one is a heart?
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `169/1326`
* **Method**: Prob = ($^{13}C_1 \times ^{13}C_1$) / $^{52}C_2$.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Favorable ways = 13 * 13 = 169. Total ways = 1326. Prob = 169/1326 = 13/102.
* **Target Time**: 50 seconds
</details>
