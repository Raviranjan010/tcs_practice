## [TCS NQT] [2023] [Ninja] [Medium] Question 1

What is the probability of getting at least one head when three coins are tossed?

A) 7/8
B) 1/8
C) 3/8
D) 1/2

**Answer**: `A`

### Explanation:
1. Total outcomes = 2^3 = 8.
2. Favorable = 1 - P(no heads) = 1 - P(TTT) = 1 - 1/8 = 7/8.
