**Q11.** If the Cost Price of 13 articles is equal to the Selling Price of 11 articles, find the profit or loss percent.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `18.18% Profit`
* **Method**: CP of X = SP of Y => SP/CP = X/Y. Calculate profit/loss.
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  SP/CP = 13/11. Profit = 2. Profit% = (2/11)*100 = 18.18% Profit.
* **Target Time**: 35 seconds
</details>

## 🔴 Advanced Level (Questions 21 - 30)

**Q21.** A shopkeeper marks up his goods by 31% and allows a discount of 10%. Find the ratio of Cost Price to Marked Price.
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `100 : 131`
* **Method**: Use relation CP/MP = (100 - Discount)/(100 + Markup).
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Let CP = 100. Then MP = 100 + 31. Ratio = 100 : 131.
* **Target Time**: 30 seconds
</details>
