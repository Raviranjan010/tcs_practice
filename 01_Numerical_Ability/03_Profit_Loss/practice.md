## 🟢 Beginner Level (Questions 1 - 15)

**Q1.** An article is purchased for Rs. 150 and sold for Rs. 170. Find the profit percentage.
A) A) 13.33%
B) B) 15.33%
C) C) 11.33%
D) D) 17.33%
<details>
<summary>Click to view Answer & Explanation</summary>

* **Answer**: `A`
* **Method**: Profit Percentage Formula
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
  Profit = SP - CP = 170 - 150 = Rs. 20.
  Profit % = (Profit / CP) * 100 = (20 / 150) * 100 = 13.33%.
* **Target Time**: 45 seconds
</details>

* **Method**: Profit Percentage Formula
* **Step-by-Step Solution**:
